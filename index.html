Ship

An arcade style top scrolling game in HTML5. A work in progress..

tomwadley.net/ship

  • Use the Arrow Keys to move (You can also use left click)
  • Press Control to shoot.
  • Press P to pause (and again to resume)
  • Press S to toggle sound (experimental: you may need a beta or nightly build of your browser for good results)
  • Press R to toggle free range mode. Makes it harder but it's fun.

Ship is on github: https://github.com/tomwadley/ship
The version playable here is probably the latest commit on the master branch
